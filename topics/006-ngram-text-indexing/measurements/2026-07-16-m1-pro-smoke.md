## Measurement boundary

Each timer spans 160 queries after four complete warm-up queries. It excludes
process startup, deterministic corpus generation, index construction, warm-up,
candidate-count reporting, and output. The index path includes posting probes
and exact verification. The scan path includes exact matching across every
document.
